Kate Wright

"Be you, the world will adjust."

I am a student of Video Game design and development, finally following my passion.
I firmly believe that our passions; the things that excite us, the things that we are curious about, the things that on a daily/weekly bases we choose to make time in our busy schedules to do because they bring a smile to our face and light our souls on fire; those are some of the most important things to pursue in life.

I was recently faced with a huge, some would say life changing, decision and it wasn’t until I took my passions into consideration that I realized the decision was easy....

My husband and I moved to Germany in 2017 and because of this move I wasn't going to be able to complete my Nursing degree, and surprisingly I wasn’t upset about it at all... Turns out I felt more of an obligation to get my Nursing degree than a passion for it. So I asked myself, what am I passionate about?

I have since embarked on an entirely different career path and I couldn’t be more excited to turn a passion of mine into a lifelong career.
